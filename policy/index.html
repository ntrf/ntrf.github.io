Cookie Policy

This website should not store any cookies

The HTTP content serving service, which you’re currently using (“My Website”) to fetch and view this page, does not have any need to store any identification of its users. I’ve done my best to get rid of all of the cookies on My Website form both first-party and third-party sources, or any other identification technology. There is no cookie consent for this reason. If some functionality of My Website might require storing such identifying information, a cookie consent will be displayed, which may or may not be followed by the usage of cookies if accepted.

The reason i’m completely removing all cookies from My Website are simple:

  • I don’t host any advertisements,
  • I’m not advertising my pages,
  • I don’t believe counting views and session times can help me improve My Website,
  • I want to comply with local and international laws with lest amount of effort,
  • I don’t trust third-party service providers, regardless of benefits,
  • I hate seeing “consent boxes” on all other websites.

Third-party resources like fonts and scripts

I’m in the process of eliminating all the third-party sources, but it’s going to take a while. Luckily, most third-party resources can be cached and can not be tracked, which makes them much safer that cookies. If you are concerned about third-party scripts tracking you, i can suggest installing extensions such as Decentraleyes.

Share buttons

I can add share buttons for FB and other social networks, but most of the time they come with privacy concerns. For the time being share buttons are removed.

What if any of this is not true?

If you find out that any of the cookies are stored without your consent, you need to check if you’re not having content injected by browser extensions of your ISP. Try to use a clean web browser installation on a virus-free machine and see if problem persists. If you’re certain, that none of your actions help, please Contact me as there might be a security breach of some sort.
